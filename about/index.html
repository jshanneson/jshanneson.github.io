A graduate from University of Waterloo's Nanotechnology Engineering program, Joshua has a deep understanding of material science, nanoelectronics, and microfabrication techniques. He has experience with material synthesis and characterization, working with equipment such as SEM, XRD, and UV-Vis Spectroscopy for examining material properties. He is trained in object-oriented programming, numerical schemes for differential equation solvers, and using statistical mechanics to simulate particle trajectories. His professional expertise encompasses process engineering, Lean manufacturing, continuous improvement, UX design and marketing, and data analytics.

As the ex-President of Waterloo's Juggling Club, Joshua has a passion for all things circus arts, and regularly attends juggling conventions around North America. He is also an accomplished digital artist and animator, having explored 3D modeling tools like Blender and Maya for many years and now working on video game development and digital painting as well. Joshua is a certified classical pianist / trumpeter and has spent 5 years singing and beatboxing with a competitive a cappella team. He enjoys discussing music theory and production, has a deep love for jazz, and often carries around a harmonica.
